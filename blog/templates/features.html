Features

Enjoy Your Property Management with Our Easy, affordable and User-Friendly Software! Keep track of everything that happens in your properties all in one place. Say goodbye to stress and paperwork, say hello to simplicity.

Rent Collection:

Simplify the rent collection process with our secure and convenient payment system. Accept payments online, set up recurring payments, and track payment history to reduce administrative burden and improve cash flow.

Accounting:

Effortlessly manage your finances with our comprehensive accounting tools. Track income, expenses, and generate financial reports to ensure your property’s profitability and compliance.

Rent Savings/Wallet:

Help your tenants manage their finances better with our rent savings and wallet feature. Tenants can save towards their rent, budget their expenses, and view their savings progress all in one place.

Maintenance Management:

Stay on top of maintenance and repairs with our repair tracking feature. Log repair requests, schedule maintenance tasks, and monitor the status of repairs to ensure issues are resolved promptly and efficiently, keeping your property in top condition.

Visitor Management:

Enhance security and improve visitor experience with our visitor management system. Pre-register guests, track visitor entries and exits, and maintain a secure environment for your residents.

Dashboards:

Stay on top of your operations with our dashboards. Visualize key metrics and data in real-time to make informed decisions quickly and efficiently.

Team Management:

Streamline team collaboration and management. Assign tasks, monitor progress, and communicate effectively with your team to ensure smooth property operations and enhanced productivity.

Marketing:

Attract and retain tenants with our robust marketing tools. Create and manage listings, promote special offers, and analyze campaign performance to optimize your marketing strategies.

Forum:

Foster community engagement with our built-in forum feature. Property owners, managers, and residents can communicate, share updates, and discuss important topics in a structured, easy-to-navigate platform.

Notifications:

Keep everyone informed with automated notifications. Whether it’s payment reminders, maintenance updates, or important announcements, our notification system ensures timely and effective communication.
